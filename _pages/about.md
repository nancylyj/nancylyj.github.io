I am a PhD student in [DigiHealth Lab](https://digihealth.ucsd.edu/) at University of California, San Diego, advised by [Edward Wang](https://www.ejaywang.com/).

Before joining UCSD, I got my MS at University of Washington Seattle, advised by [Shwetak Patel](https://people.ece.uw.edu/patel_shwetak/). I was also fortunate to work with [Vikram Iyer](https://homes.cs.washington.edu/~vsiyer/). My work at [UW Ubicomp Lab](https://ubicomplab.cs.washington.edu/) focuses on wearable devices and multimodal physiological sensing.

My research lies at the intersection of embedded system and machine learning, with an emphasis in healthcare. I am interested in improving sensing technologies and developing intelligent end-to-end systems to provide ubiquitous assistants to human beings.

Please feel free to contact me at nyjliu at ucsd dot edu!
