I am a master student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Washington, advised by [Prof. Shwetak Patel](https://people.ece.uw.edu/patel_shwetak/). 

My research at [UW Ubicomp Lab](https://ubicomplab.cs.washington.edu/) lies at the intersection of embedded system and machine learning, with an emphasis in healthcare. Specifically, my work focuses on wearable devices and multimodal physiological sensing. I am interested in improving sensing technologies and developing intelligent end-to-end systems to provide ubiquitous assistants to human beings.
